Build your organisation

Every report, reporting line, and assessment in RampIQ is built on your org chart. Define it once — departments, cost centers, job titles, and the managers people report to — and everything downstream stays accurate. Two minutes, and it only happens once.

Connections

Import from Excel / Google Sheets

No setup or credentials needed. While Workday access is pending, bring in a roster straight from a spreadsheet — upload a file, or paste a Google Sheet's published CSV link. Available as a source when you create a team.

Roles & access

Every job title maps to a band. Bands decide what a person can see — their level and below, never sideways or up. Define your own titles; the bands stay universal.

Job titles → bands

Change a title's band and everyone with that title inherits the new access.

Band 1 grants full Assignee powers — multiple people can hold it. Your master profile is always Band 1 and can't be locked out.
